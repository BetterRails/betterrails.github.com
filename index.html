Better Rails

Working towards a better rails world for all...

Better Rails is in the process of becoming a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization that exists to help better the Ruby on Rails ecosystem. It's 2 short term objectives are:

  1. Provide an open source, free hosted service for monitoring and measuring the performance of individual Rails applications
  2. Provide an open source, free hosted service for error reporting of individual Rails applications
These are still in the very early concept stages, but the vision is there.

Background

Better Rails is being headed up by Mark McSpadden. (That's me.) I am very grateful for all that people have contributed to Rails, and all that Rails has done for me, but there are a few corners of the ecosystem where I think we can do better. This organization is setup to help with that vision.
